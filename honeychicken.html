Honey Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

DESCRIPTION:

The name of the dish is Honey Garlic slow cooker chicken thighs this dish ha many nutritinal values comparedto other chicken dishes.

This dish has made to top in the culinary world due to its exclusive taste.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

STEPS:

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
